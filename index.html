Introduction

Most people when they think of typewriters they might think of the times when there were no such thing as computers. Back then people used typewriters as a writing tool to type up documents such as school papers, letters, and business documents. What many people donâ€™t realize is that typewriters can be used as a creative tool to draw pictures and make patterns.

Intertwined is a thesis that explores this tool in a creative way instead of as a tool for formal communication. You will notice that I have overlapped letters to create different tonal values of color and make abstract shapes. I also explored adding color to some of the compositions. Throughout the pages of this book is my process for my senior graphic design thesis project at Montserrat College of Art.

Fall 2016 Work

The work that I produced in the Fall of 2016 in design seminar is what brought me to where I am today with this project. During that semester I became interested in using typewriters. That is when I began to work on my typewriter drawing experiments on notepad paper. I eventually worked on a peice of old paper to further my experimentation and pattern making.
